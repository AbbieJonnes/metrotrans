MetroPass is a digital transport subscription system that allows passengers to register online, subscribe to transport plans, make digital payments, and receive QR-code tickets instantly.

Start Registration

How MetroPass Works

Follow these simple steps to use the system.

Register

Create your MetroPass account by entering your details and preferred route.

Pay

Choose a weekly or monthly subscription and complete payment securely.

Receive QR

Instantly receive your digital QR ticket after successful payment.

Board

Show the QR ticket to the conductor for quick verification and boarding.

Why MetroPass?

Faster Boarding

QR verification speeds up passenger entry and reduces delays.

Secure Payments

Digital payments provide safer and more reliable transactions.

Saves Time

No more long queues or handling physical cash every day.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I subscribe?

Register on the platform, choose a plan, and complete payment to activate your pass.

Is the payment secure?

Yes. MetroPass uses secure digital payment systems for safe transactions.

What happens after payment?

A QR-code ticket is generated instantly for verification during boarding.

About MetroPass

Transforming Public
Transport Through Technology

MetroPass is a smart transport subscription platform designed to simplify urban travel. The system allows passengers to register digitally, subscribe to weekly or monthly plans, make secure payments, and receive QR-code-based tickets instantly.

Our goal is to reduce transport delays, eliminate cash-handling challenges, and improve convenience for both passengers and transport operators.
